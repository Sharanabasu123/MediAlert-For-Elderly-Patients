About MediAlert

MediAlert helps patients and caregivers manage medication schedules reliably. It combines timely reminders, clear on-screen and audible alerts, and caregiver reporting so families can reduce missed doses and act quickly when something goes wrong.

Overview

MediAlert is a lightweight medication management platform built for home and caregiver workflows. It gives patients simple, accessible prompts at scheduled times and provides caretakers with consolidated reports, notifications, and easy ways to monitor adherence. The platform is intentionally low-friction to encourage adoption: minimal setup, clear alerts, and fallback delivery to an outbox when SMTP or external services are not configured.

Why this project matters

Missed or incorrect doses are a common cause of avoidable harm, especially among older adults and people with complex medication regimens. By combining scheduled reminders, audible repetition, and a visual full-screen prompt at the scheduled time, MediAlert increases the chance that time-sensitive medications are taken correctly. Caretakers receive timely summaries and action notifications so they can step in when needed.

Advantages

  • Simple setup: Works with a basic account and a small device — no complex integrations required.
  • Multi-channel alerts: On-screen full-screen notices, repeated audible alerts, and plain-text emails (or SMS via gateway) ensure messages reach recipients.
  • Caregiver visibility: Consolidated reports and immediate action notifications keep caretakers informed.
  • Privacy conscious: Data is stored locally in the app database and role-based access limits exposure of patient details.
  • Resilient delivery: Email fallback to an `outbox/` file preserves messages when SMTP is not configured.
  • Accessible: Clear typography, large buttons, and repeatable audio make the app usable for people with diverse needs.

Key features

Scheduled reminders

Reminders sent at 5 minutes, 1 minute, and on-time to reduce missed doses.

Full-screen alerts

On-time reminders show a visual overlay that is hard to miss.

Repeated audio

Audible alerts repeat to overcome brief distractions or device sleep.

Reports

Daily/weekly adherence summaries for caretakers, exportable via email.

Action logging

Taken/missed actions are logged with the actor and time for audit and review.

Emergency alerts

Quick emergency page sends messages to assigned caretakers immediately.

Getting started

  1. Create a patient account and add basic contact details.
  2. Add medicines with schedule times (use the AM/PM helper if needed).
  3. Enable sound alerts from your device and test using the "Test Alert" button on the medicine form.
  4. Add caretakers so they receive reports and notifications.

Need help? Use the Feedback page or the Emergency page for urgent notifications.
